About School

St. Doricas Secondary School is mixed-gender school located in Nyamagana, Mwanza hosting students for both O-level and A-level. As stated, the school gives education for both O-Level and A-level, with subjects ranging for both of the levels. It has a very good reputation in the education industry, as it has had impressive performances for five (5) years running.
